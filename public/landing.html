FAQ

Common questions

Is this actual legal advice? +
No. LexAI is an AI-powered analysis tool designed to help lawyers work faster. It does not replace legal judgment and should always be reviewed by a qualified attorney before acting on any analysis.
What file types does LexAI support? +
LexAI supports PDF, Word (.docx), and plain text (.txt) files. You can also paste contract text directly into the tool.
Is my data secure? +
Your documents are processed securely and are never stored or used to train AI models. Each analysis is processed in real time and discarded immediately after.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel anytime with no questions asked. If you join at the founding price, that rate is locked in for as long as you remain a member.

Document Drafter, Client Intake, and Deadline Tracker are actively in development. Founding members get early access to every new feature as it launches.
